Calculator

Damage calculation made simple.


Have you ever had trouble comparing two similar weapons? Or knowing when it's worth using an element-focused weapon instead of something with more raw damage? What about armor skills? Do you fret over taking Critical Eye instead of Attack Up? Or would you just like to know how much all of those attack boosters actually help?


Well I have the perfect thing for you! As a labor of love and curiosity, I have built a calculator to answer all of the above questions-- and more! However, it's still a work in progress. So please leave some feedback if you run into any issues. You can notfiy me via the contact page or on Github.

Weapons

How do you like to play?


Do you enjoy being quick and reactive?

Or maybe you're methodical and preemptive?

Perhaps you'd rather take a more stylish approach?


Regardless of your preferred playstyle, there is sure to be a weapon type for you. Instead of the traditional blademaster/gunner split: they are now categorized as Light, Heavy, and Technical. No weapon type is neccessarily stronger than another. So your skill level will often be the most important factor. I urge you to try every type, as each one has a distinct playstyle. Not to mention that it can be useful to have a few different options, depending on the Monster that you're facing.
